## Transliteration Notes

- KMU 2010 standard is used for Ukrainian proper nouns (e.g., Наддніпрянщина → Naddniprianshchyna) unless a well-established English form exists
- Well-established English forms are preferred for internationally known names: Kyiv (not Kyїv), Herodotus (not Herodot), Scythians (not Skify)
- Archaeological culture names follow the most common English-language academic usage
- Latin/Greek terms preserved as-is: *de facto*, *Homo sapiens*, *anima*
